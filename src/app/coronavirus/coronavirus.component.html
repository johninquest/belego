Coronavirus safety recommendations for drivers and transporters
  • Keep vehicle ventilated at all times (if possible).
  • Increase hygiene and sanitation. Clean and sanitize vehicle (especially seats, armsets, doors and handles) after every trip.
  • Drivers should use face masks and sanitary gloves (if possible).
  • Avoid overloading. Underloading in order to achieve some amount of social distancing between passengers is more effective.
  • Avoid cash if possible (mobile money is an alternative).
  • Only accept passengers with signs of a cold or fever if necessary.
  • Keep records of your passengers. In case one of them eventually turns out to be an infected person, those records can help prevent further infections.
  • Use posters inside and outside of vehicles to make passengers aware of the situation.
General coronavirus (COVID-19) safety recommendations by the WHO
  1. Wash your hands often.
  2. Cough / sneeze into your elbow.
  3. Don't touch your face.
  4. Stay more than 1 meter away from each other.
  5. Stay home if you feel sick.
