stuuurbeta - ist eine kostenlose Webanwendung um Schnappschusse von kleine Transaktionen zu generieren.

Highlights

  • Schnell: loads fast and is very responsive to user interactions.
  • Unkompliziert: generate proof of transaction (invoice) in just 3 straightforward steps.
  • Datenschutz: all entered information used to generate proof of transaction (invoice) automatically gets deleted when the browser is closed.

Anleitung

  1. Start app by clicking on the "LOS LEGEN" button.
  2. Choose the type of transaction you would like to document; "VERSCHENKEN" or "VERKAUFEN".
  3. Enter transaction information (e.g. items, prices, date and place of transaction).
  4. Enter persons information (e.g. Käufer/Verkäufer or Geber/Empfänger).
  5. Click on the "VORSCHAU" button to preview the captured information.
  6. Click on the "SPEICHERN" button to generate and save an image of the documented transaction.
  7. Close the browser when you are done.
