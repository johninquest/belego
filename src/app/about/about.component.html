stuuurbeta - is a simple web tool for capturing small person to person transactions.

Highlights

  • Lightweight: loads fast and is very responsive to user interactions.
  • Simple: generate proof of transaction (invoice) in just 3 straightforward steps.
  • Private: all entered information used to generate proof of transaction (invoice) automatically gets deleted when the browser is closed.

How to use

  1. Start app by clicking on the "GET STARTED" button.
  2. Choose the type of transaction you would like to document; "GIVE AWAY" or "SALE.
  3. Enter transaction information (e.g. items, prices, date and place of transaction).
  4. Enter persons information (e.g. buyer/seller or giver/receiver).
  5. Click on the "PREVIEW" button to preview the captured information.
  6. Click on the "SAVE" button to generate and save an image of the documented transaction.
  7. Close the browser when you are done.
