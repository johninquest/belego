ROAD SAFETY
(useful tips)
  • Avoid speeding.
  • Avoid driving while tired. Get some rest.
  • Avoid overcrowding your vehicle.
  • Be vigilant while driving for blind spots and other reckless drivers. Slow down if necessary.
  • Do not drink and drive.
  • Do not drive too close to the vehicle ahead. Always leave space for a safe braking distance.
  • Make sure your headlights are working. You will need them in the dark and when driving through a fog.
  • Pay attention to road signs.
  • Put your phone away and avoid other distractions while driving.
  • Slow down before taking corners.
  • Watch out for potholes and slow down well in advance.
  • Wear your seat belt.
